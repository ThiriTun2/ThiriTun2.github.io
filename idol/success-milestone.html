Kaung Myat San, born on December 14, 1993, in Mandalay, Myanmar, is a prominent Burmese actor and model. He graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English from Yadanabon University.

In 2012, Kaung Myat San moved to Yangon and participated in the "King of Denim 2012" contest. He also competed in MRTV-4's casting competition for the series "The Sign of Love" and
was selected for a role, marking his acting debut.

Throughout his career, he has been recognized for his roles in several television series, including:
